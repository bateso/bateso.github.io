WHAT WE DO:

Here at Amazin' Glazin' we do our very best to get you the best quality donuts at a price that you can afford! We treat our customers with the up most respect. The customer is always right! We strive to be polite and respectful of everyone that comes into our store. We are based out of Hershey PA, the sweetest place on Earth! Our motto here at Amazin' Glazin' is donut worry, be happy. Our goal is to satisfy the customers wants so you do not have to worry all you need to do it be happy. 10% of all profits will be donated to Meals On Wheels and Kids Power Packs to end hunger here in America. So come and visit us! We would love to serve you!

OWNER:

OLIVIA BATES:

Olivia Bates is the founder and owner of Amazin' Glazin'. She enjoys traveling around the world and trying different types of food from each place she goes to. She is extremely passionate when it comes to food, but definitely has the greatest passion when it comes to donuts. She started Amazin' Glazin' to pursue her dreams in being a pastry chef and running her own business. One of her goals is to get everyone to appreciate food just like she does. She loves eating just as much as she does baking. She is also passionate about adults and children going hungry here in America. So she has set up that 10% of all profits will go to ending hunger. Our partners for ending this hunger are Meals On Wheels and Kids Power Packs. She genuinely just wants to better the life of people who need help the most in this world. So come down and buy a donut or two and help change the world!
