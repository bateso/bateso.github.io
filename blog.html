Come support Compassion International!!

THIS IS WRITTEN BY OLIVIA ON MARCH 20, 2019:

I just got back from my vacation to Rwanda. I went to Rwanda to support a mission called Compassion International. Compassion International is a mission where children from impoverished countries are supported by people who are more fortunate then themselves. The people sponsoring their compassion child pay for all the child's medical needs and an education through 12th grade. I was partnering with them for this trip and it was so much fun! I loved getting to know all the children and parents through this great mission. I am going to be putting up pieces of papers with children's pictures and a little bit about them on a bulletin board at the Amazin' Glazin' store so you can sponsor a child yourself. All you have to do is grab the child's picture that you want to sponsor and show it to the front counter. Then you will receive a letter in the mail telling you what all it means to support a child. So come down to Amazin' Glazin' and come support a Compassion Child for yourself!
